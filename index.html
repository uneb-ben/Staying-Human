What Remains After Speaking

About the Writer

A lifelong reader.
A compulsive writer.

This site is a home for everything I feel compelled to put into words — opinions on the world as I find it, careful readings of books I love (or argue with), occasional academic work, personal essays, poetry, and whatever else doesn't fit anywhere.

I believe that writing is how we think, not just how we record thought. Everything here is an attempt to understand something a little better.
